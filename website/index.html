PRIVACY

What we don't see.

Most "focus apps" sold to companies are surveillance products in a wellness wrapper. 45 isn't. Here is precisely what your employer cannot get from us, because we don't have it:

  • Which sites you visit. We see the root domain (e.g. linkedin.com) only at the moment a blocking decision is made. We don't log it, store it, or report it.
  • What you wrote in your task description. Sent to OpenAI as part of the per-domain check, then discarded. Never persisted on our end.
  • Whether you actually worked. The camera presence check runs entirely on your machine. We never see the video stream and no images leave your laptop.
  • How many sprints you completed. Stored locally on your machine. Aggregate count for the team is uploaded once a day, with no per-user breakdown.
  • Your tasks, history, or settings. All on your machine in plain JSON files you can read or delete anytime.

FAQ

Why locked at 4 × 45 minutes? Can I customize it?

No. The constraint is the feature. Every focus app with a "duration slider" eventually becomes an app you negotiate with. 45 doesn't negotiate.

Does my employer see what I do?

No. All AI evaluation, camera detection, and process monitoring runs locally on your machine. The only thing 45 sends to a server is the domain name being judged — never page content, never URLs, never employee identity.

What if my camera is off / unavailable?

You can disable presence detection in Settings. Sprints still block sites and apps, you just don't get the "walk away → forfeit" check.

What if I need a 5th sprint?

You don't. Tomorrow.

Does it work on Mac / Linux?

Windows only right now. Mac is on the roadmap.

What's the AI? Where does my task description go?

OpenAI's gpt-4o-mini. Your task string + the domain being judged go in one short prompt. No URLs, no page content, no history. Costs roughly $0.001 per sprint.

(HR) Does this surveil our employees?

No. 45 is a self-imposed commitment tool. The only data point we ever upload to a server is a root domain name at the moment of a blocking decision — and we don't store it. The camera presence check runs locally; we never see the video. Aggregate usage stats (sprints completed across the team) are the only thing visible to a team admin, and never per-user.

(HR) Does it work with our works council / German labor law?

Yes — because 45 collects no identifiable employee data and runs entirely on the employee's machine, it sits outside most workplace-surveillance regulations. Happy to provide our security/privacy one-pager for your council review.

(HR) How do we deploy this to 50 people?

Send a signed installer link. They run it, sign in with their work email, done. No agent, no MDM profile, no IT ticket. Seats are managed via a simple web dashboard you'll get with your team subscription.

(HR) What happens when someone leaves?

Revoke their seat from the dashboard. Their app stops working within 24 hours; their local task history stays on their personal machine (we never had it).

(HR) Can we get a custom DPA / MSA?

Yes for team subscriptions of 25+ seats. Email hello@45.day with your standard template.
